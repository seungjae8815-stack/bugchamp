[Full description] (App Store & Google Play)
🪲 Collect, raise, and battle your very own bugs!

Rhino beetles, stag beetles, mantises, longhorns… gather a wild variety of insects and build the ultimate team in this idle battler. Your bugs keep foraging even when the game is closed.

■ Idle collecting
Bugs auto-explore habitats and hunt bosses. Rewards pile up for up to 8 hours while you're away.

■ Every bug is one of a kind
No two bugs are the same — not even two rhino beetles.
· Element (Wood · Fire · Earth · Metal · Water)
· Size — rolled on a normal distribution; bigger is stronger
· Potential 1–5★ — sets the enhancement cap
· Temperament — its behavior in battle
· Sex — a breeding condition

■ Battles that reward thinking
This isn't a plain stat race.
· Three stances — Attack / Defend / Heal (Attack > Heal > Defend > Attack)
· Energy management — no infinite defending or healing
· Elemental clash deals 1.5× damage
· Team order IS the strategy — when a front bug empowers the one behind, the whole team grows stronger
· Each battlefield favors a different element
In manual battles you read your opponent move by move — pure mind games.

■ Breed stronger champions
Pair adult males and females to get eggs, then raise them in the incubator. A child inherits its parents' size and potential, with a rare chance of a mutation. Crafting great bugs is the heart of the game.

■ Battle other players
Face other players' defense teams asynchronously. Earn trophies, climb from Bronze to Diamond, and compete for rank each season.

■ Great for you if you…
· love insects
· want an idle game with real depth of progression
· want to win by team-building and order, not luck

■ Notes
· Most content is playable offline (ranking & PvP need a connection)
· Ads are opt-in rewarded ads only — never forced
· Paid items focus on convenience and cosmetics; stats are never sold directly
